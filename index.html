ABOUT ME
       

Hello! I'm Grace Abunyie, a passionate data analyst currently completing a Post Graduate course in Business Insights and Analytics. With a foundation in Psychology, acquired through both a Diploma (2013) and a Bachelor's Degree (2016), I bring a unique perspective to the intersection of business and data. I thrive on unraveling the intricacies of both numbers and human behavior, aspiring to blend the analytical prowess gained in my academic pursuits with a deep understanding of how the human mind works. My mission is to contribute to and transform the business world with insights that not only make sense statistically but resonate with the human touch. Join me as I navigate through the realms of data, bridging the gap between business strategy and the intricacies of the human psyche.
